AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETHAM

AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETHAM (AMRITA UNIVERSITY) IS A PREMIER PRIVATE DEEMED-TO-BE-UNIVERSITY IN INDIA KNOWN FOR ITS STRONG EMPHASIS ON RESEARCH, VALUE-BASED EDUCATION, AND HIGH ACADEMIC RANKINGS. FOUNDED BY SPIRITUAL LEADER MATA AMRITANANDAMAYI (AMMA) IN 1994, IT HAS GROWN INTO A MULTI-CAMPUS MULTI-DISCIPLINARY INSTITUTION WITH A SIGNIFICANT NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL REPUTATION.
